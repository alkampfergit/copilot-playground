# Standard Enemies

## Goblin
- **Strength**: 2-4
- **Intelligence**: 1-3
- **Dexterity**: 3-5
- **Charisma**: 1-2
- **Endurance**: 2-4
- **Description**: Small and cunning creatures, often attack in groups.

## Orc
- **Strength**: 5-7
- **Intelligence**: 2-4
- **Dexterity**: 3-5
- **Charisma**: 2-3
- **Endurance**: 6-8
- **Description**: Strong and aggressive, known for their brute force.

## Skeleton
- **Strength**: 3-5
- **Intelligence**: 1-2
- **Dexterity**: 4-6
- **Charisma**: 0
- **Endurance**: 3-5
- **Description**: Undead creatures animated by dark magic.

## Dragon
- **Strength**: 8-10
- **Intelligence**: 7-9
- **Dexterity**: 6-8
- **Charisma**: 5-7
- **Endurance**: 9-10
- **Description**: Powerful and fearsome, capable of breathing fire.
